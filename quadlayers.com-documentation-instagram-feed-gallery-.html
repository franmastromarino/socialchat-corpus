1. Features

Instagram Feed Gallery offers many features and advantages to integrate your social feed:

1: Simplified image upload.

2:Recovers user’s data such as likes and comments from your feed.

3: Helps you keep your site up-to-date, getting new feeds from Instagram.

4: It’s compatible with any theme.

5: Responsive and mobile optimized.

6: Allows multiple Instagram feeds with no limitations.

7:Reliable data caching.

8: Different built-in layout to display the feeds: grid, carousel, masonry, and highlight to display your content in any way you want.

9: Pop-up media lightbox to display photos and videos from your feed in a beautiful pop-up lightbox which allows users to experience your content without leaving your site.

10: Built-in customization options that let you tweak your Instagram feed.
