Best WooCommerce Gift Card Plugins (Free and Premium)

April 11, 2023
|In Plugins
|By sajan

Are you looking for the best WooCommerce gift card plugins to create and sell gift vouchers on your online store? This article will list the top tools you should try.

Gift vouchers are an excellent strategy to increase sales and build client loyalty in WooCommerce. You can quickly create and sell them on your online store the way you want with the correct plugin. But before we go through the actual list of the plugins, you need to understand the benefits of gift cards in WooCommerce first.

Benefits of Gift Cards in WooCommerce

There are various advantages to offering gift vouchers, which include:

  • Boost sales: Gift cards can be used in your online store to encourage customers to spend more money on your products. They may buy gift cards as gifts for friends and family, which will increase the sales for your store.
  • Attract new customers: The customers that received the gift cards may not have previously visited your store. So they are more inclined to do so now that they have a gift card to utilize on your website.
  • Improve customer loyalty: Customers who receive vouchers as gifts are more likely to return to your store to redeem them. This can help in developing a long-term relationship with your customers.
  • Increase revenue: There is a slight chance that customers who purchased the gift cards might not use the card’s total value in some cases. This means your store can keep the unused fund as a profit.
  • Customer convenience: Gift cards are an extremely convenient and flexible gifting option that simplifies gift-giving. Firstly, the customers can choose the amount they want to spend by purchasing a gift card. Then, the recipients can purchase the products they genuinely wish.
  • Customizable options: Gift vouchers in WooCommerce can be modified to match your store’s branding and appearance. You can customize the appearance, add unique messages or images, and set expiration dates or other restrictions.

These benefits show that gift vouchers are very profitable for your online store. So let’s see why we use plugins to add the gift cards in WooCommerce too.

Why Use Gift Card Plugins in WooCommerce?

Using the WooCommerce gift card plugins is the easiest way to create gift cards for your online store. They provide all the necessary features while offering gift cards to customers. These features mainly include the customizable options we mentioned earlier, but there are other helpful features too.

Most of these plugins provide analytics and reporting options to track your gift cards and inventory. This can even help you to understand how your business is performing and make informed decisions about your marketing strategy.

The plugins are also straightforward to install and use. So overall, using the right gift card plugin will help you experience all the benefits of gift cards in your store.

Best WooCommerce Gift Card Plugins

Our list of the best gift card plugins for WooCommerce includes the following:

  1. PW WooCommerce Gift Cards (Freemium)
  2. Gift Up! (Freemium)
  3. WooCommerce PDF Vouchers (Premium)
  4. Ultimate Gift Cards for WooCommerce (Freemium)
  5. YITH WooCommerce Gift Cards (Freemium)

We’ll thoroughly review these plugins, including their highlighting features and pricing options.

Without any further ado, let’s get into the list.

1) PW WooCommerce Gift Cards

PW WooCommerce Gift Cards is the most popular plugin that can help you start selling gift cards in no time. Its ease of use, configuration options, and wide range of customization options are significant reasons for its popularity.

PW WooCommerce Gift Cards offers your customers a straightforward purchasing process where they can specify the amount, recipient, and message while completing the order. Your customers will receive an email with the voucher information as soon as the payment is made. You can also customize the email template so you can design it according to your preferences or occasions.

The tool is also compatible with most themes. This means there won’t be any conflicts or compatibility issues in your store. Your customers can spend their vouchers during the checkout process. The plugin will automatically apply the balance after tax, displaying the updated balance on the cart and checkout page.

Features

  • Flexible purchasing options for customers
  • Customizable email templates
  • Allow customers to set amounts, recipients, and message
  • Receive gift vouchers without creating an account as a guest
  • Compatible with multiple languages

Pricing

PW WooCommerce Gift Cards is a freemium plugin. You can download the free version from the official WordPress plugin repository. The premium version starts at 99 USD annually for one site license and includes one year of updates and support.

2) Gift Up!

Gift Up! is an excellent solution to design and sell gift cards using WooCommerce and your social media networks. It is one of the simplest ways to sell gift vouchers online, with no monthly fees or startup charges. You only have to pay a small price to the plugin when you sell a gift card successfully.

Gift Up! helps you create unique cards and certificates by personalizing them with your brand and logo. You can select from various unique and stunning designs to customize your order. If this is not enough for you, you can even design your voucher from the ground up.

It also allows you an option in which the customers can set custom amounts or use specific pre-set amounts you add. As soon as the purchase is made with the selected price, you will receive the voucher instantly. The tool supports debit, credit cards, and even Apple Pay and Google Pay for the payment

Features

  • Make one-of-a-kind  designs that match your brand and business
  • Automated distribution with immediate or scheduled delivery
  • Checkout shortcodes
  • Specify currency and expiry dates
  • Fully responsive design suitable for all mobile devices

Pricing

Gift Up! is initially a free plugin. You can download the plugin from the official WordPress plugin repository, but you must pay a 3.49% flat fee for every sale.

3) WooCommerce PDF Vouchers

If you need one of the best feature-rich premium plugins, WooCommerce PDF Vouchers is also a suitable tool. It is ideal for businesses that want to quickly give customers gift vouchers, certificates, promotions, event tickets, or awards. This plugin lets you produce infinite PDF coupons that customers can redeem online or in person.

You get a drag-and-drop builder and a huge variety of templates to create the exact type of vouchers without any hassle. The tool also supports barcodes and QR codes, adding more flexibility for your customers while redeeming. Furthermore, customers can customize their vouchers on the store’s front end, which can be a great convenience.

Apart from these customer-based options, you also get some powerful admin and reporting features that make it easy to manage your business and track sales. This can help maintain a smooth operation and outstanding customer service in your online store.

Features

  • Create and design unlimited gift vouchers
  • Multiple gift voucher templates for any eCommerce store
  • Additional support for barcodes and QR codes
  • Allow customers to design their cards
  • Powerful admin options to report and track voucher codes

Pricing

WooCommerce PDF Vouchers is a premium plugin. You can purchase the plugin from CodeCanyon, priced at 45 USD. This includes future updates and six months of support.

4) Ultimate Gift Cards for WooCommerce

The Ultimate Gift Cards for WooCommerce plugin is also a good choice for creating, selling, and managing digital and physical gift vouchers on your online store. You can use this plugin to modify gift vouchers using predefined templates and deliver them via email or downloadable PDFs. Apart from that, they can also be designed and sold in your native language.

Ultimate Gift Cards for WooCommerce allows you to sell gift vouchers on your store with five different pricing types, which can have their minimum limit. They can even be displayed on the shop page or a specific card page. You can also prevent customers from using this plugin on particular products and categories with the help of the exclusion feature.

On top of that, Ultimate Gift Cards for WooCommerce allows you to enable or disable tax exemption for gift vouchers. The tax calculation can even be automated if necessary. Overall, it is a good tool.

Features

  • Set a minimum limit on vouchers based on pricing types
  • Create cards with duration-based costing with variable pricing
  • Enable, disable, or automate the tax calculation
  • Easy online and real redeeming
  • Product, category, and sale items exclusion options

Pricing

Ultimate Gift Cards for WooCommerce is a freemium plugin. You can download the free version from the official WordPress plugin repository. The premium version starts at 79 USD per year for one site license and includes one year of updates and support. Also, it comes with a 30-day money-back guarantee.

5) YITH WooCommerce Gift Cards

YITH WooCommerce Gift Cards is a feasible option if you need to start selling gift vouchers on your online store quickly. It has all the bells and whistles a gift card plugin should have. However, the fact that you can upload unlimited images in the image gallery sets it apart from the other plugins.

Moreover, the plugin has several other notable features too. You can set a specific amount for each card, a dedicated plugin dashboard, etc. The dashboard will show you all the details where you can check the balance and related orders.

YITH WooCommerce Gift Cards also lets the customers enter the recipient’s information and automatically send the order details via email as soon as the purchase is made. Furthermore, the customers can also select a preferred layout for the vouchers by choosing from a gallery of images provided by you. Similarly, you can even change the code pattern if you want to do so based on the needs of your website.

Features:

  • Admin dashboard to check the  balance, related orders, and so on
  • Allows customers to select their preferred layout
  • Customization options
  • Upload unlimited images in the image gallery
  • Let customers fill recipient’s information and send the gift card by email

Pricing

YITH WooCommerce Gift Cards is a freemium plugin. You can download the free version from the official WordPress plugin repository. The premium version starts at 149.99 Euros per year and includes one year of updates and support and a 30-day money-back guarantee.

Conclusion

This concludes our list of the best WooCommerce gift card plugins. You can use any of them to start creating and selling gift vouchers in your store. But the best plugin out of them entirely depends on the requirement of your budget, customers, and personal interest.

If you don’t want any monthly or yearly subscription while selling gift vouchers, you can use Gift Up! You can use all its features for free without paying a single dime. But they will charge you a specific fee for each purchase on your website.

However, you can try other plugins like PW WooCommerce Gift Card, Ultimate Gift Cards for WooCommerce, and YITH WooCommerce Gift Cards in case you don’t want to pay the fee. The features in the free version provided by these plugins are optimal for a small online store.

You can always upgrade to their premium version for more features.

But if you are comfortable spending a certain amount for a feature-rich premium plugin, WooCommerce PDF Vouchers is the plugin you’ll ever need.
